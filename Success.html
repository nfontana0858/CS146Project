How We Measure Success

The success of the planner website will be evaluated through usability, system performance, and user engagement.

  • 75% of users report that the interface is intuitive and easy to navigate without extra guidance.
  • The system supports up to 50 concurrent users without performance issues.
  • Page load times remain under 3 seconds.
  • 80% or more of entered tasks and goals are actively tracked and updated during a two week testing period.
