Welcome

Welcome to our Calendar Management System project. The purpose of our website is to allow users to schedule, track, manage individual/group events, goals, assignments, and more! By integrating everything in one place, the website improves users' time management, accountability, and overall organization while keeping motivation going through clear progress tracking.

Features

  • Personal scheduling and task management
  • Group planning and shared calendars
  • Goal tracking and reminders
  • Organized planning in one place
