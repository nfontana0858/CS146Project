Design Constraints

  • The interface must remain simple and easy to navigate.
  • The project follows a waterfall development process.
  • Scalability is limited to small groups due to limited resources.
  • The website must remain responsive across devices.
  • No secure login system will be implemented.
  • The design must remain minimal due to development time and experience.

Out of Scope

  • No user authentication or login system.
  • No storage of sensitive personal data.
  • No large scale enterprise deployment.
  • No native iOS or Android apps.
  • No integration with Google Calendar or other APIs.
  • No AI recommendations or advanced analytics.
